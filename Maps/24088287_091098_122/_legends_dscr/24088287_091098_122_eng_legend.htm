Zoning of forests by density of radioactive contamination of soil by cesium-137

(in accordance with recommendations on forest management under radioactive contamination conditions)
> 30 Ci/km2, development of special regime of forestry works related to fire-protection and forest-protection
15 - 30 Ci/km2, control over the duration of working time during the urgent forest-protection works
10 - 15 Ci/km2, limitation of wood utilization for other purposes
7 - 10 Ci/km2, limitation of utilization treated lumber, woods for fabrication of domestic goods and for preservation of food
5 - 7 Ci/km2, limitation of utilization of firewood and lighter trees
2 - 5 Ci/km2, limitation of utilization of medicinal plants, wild berry plants and forest hayfields
1 - 2 Ci/km2, limitation of utilization of mushrooms and some medicinal plants
Utilization of forest products without limitations

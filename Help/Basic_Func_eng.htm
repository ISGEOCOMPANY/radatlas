Search information on the map

The map search tool is activated by clicking on the button . The map search tool is shown below (Fig. 4).

Fig. 4 - The map search tool

To find an item you must:

  1. Choose the layer to search (Fig. 5)

    Fig. 5 - Layer choosing

  2. Choose the attribute to search (Fig. 6).

    Fig. 6 - Attribute choosing

    3. Search by attributes of three types, namely:

    • Text. With the introduction of the text (at least 3 characters).
    • Text and ranks string, range, (where the string - the search will be carried out in a row; range - for the range of choices). From the ranks can also select a fixed range.
    • Arithmetic. >, <, >=, <=, =, ( where >, <, >=, <=, = - conditions for the numbers) Search more, less, and equal number of attribute values.
  3. Choose the condition to search (Fig. 7).

    Fig. 7 - Condition choosing

  4. Choose the range to search (Fig. 8) or enter the value.

    Fig. 8 - Range choosing

  5. To search, click on the button . Search result will be highlighted on the map.

To clear search result, you on the button .
